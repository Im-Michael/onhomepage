SWTOR Universe Roleplay


Taking place on the Smuggler's Moon, Nar Shaddaa, you can live out a life of crime in the lower levels. Side with the Hutt Cartel, Exchange Syndicate, start a reputable business, or form a guild of your own. If those don't suit you, enlist into the military under the Galactic Republic or the Sith Empire, both operating in the cities underbelly to better their cause.
